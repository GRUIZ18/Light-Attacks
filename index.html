Light Attacks 101

What You Need To Know

What is a light attack you ask? I was hoping you would. A light attack is an insult or jab that involves no fowl or innapropriate language but instead focuses on inconviences everyone experiences. These types of remarks are extremely effective as they are often unexpected and cause no permanent damage to the relationship. These are best used against friends, parents, or perhaps to difuse a tense situation. Whatever the reason, light attacks are an easy way to get under someone's skin with out offending them. Without further ado, let's get into it.


"I hope the line is super long when you go to Walmart"

A simple yet frustrating inconvenience can be enough to send someone over the edge.



"I hope your eyes are half closed in every photo"

From family photos to LinkedIn profile pictures, not looking your best is a pet peeve that ticks off anyone and everyone.



"Your fast food order gets messed up."

Any person will tell you, when their simple burger fries with a frosty order isn't what's in the bag, a rage unlike any other ensues.
